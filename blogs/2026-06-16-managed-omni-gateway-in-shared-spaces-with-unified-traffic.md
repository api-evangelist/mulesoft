MuleSoft Omni Gateway secures and governs API, MCP, LLM, and agent traffic across deployment models including the MuleSoft-hosted Managed Omni Gateway and the customer-hosted Self Managed Omni Gateway. Customers often prefer Managed Omni Gateway for its lower overhead and automatic updates and maintenance, now supported in shared spaces with unified traffic control.
